Writing

As a Lady of Letters my published work can be summed up in three distinct categories—journalism, copywriting and academic. I consider the work I did as a journalist or a copywriter—some in which I earned a paycheck and other work that I did on a pro bono basis—as my professional work.

My academic work is just that—academic. My academic work showcases the best work I did while earning my degree at Alverno College.

You will find several examples of all three categories at this website. For more examples please email me at kaufmanjb [at] yahoo [dot] com.

For more of my writing also check out my blogs. The Book Self is my book review blog and other assorted book and writing related content. Popcorn In My Bra is my film review blog and also contains information about movies and the film industry as a whole.
